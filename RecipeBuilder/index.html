Calculating Your Brewhouse Efficiency

There are a number of references that specify what Brewhouse Efficiency is and how to calculate it. At Fuge, our definition and calculation of Brewhouse efficiency may be different than some references. Brewhouse Efficiency is how efficient your brewhouse is at extracting sugar from the grain. To do that, you can make adjustments to the mill, the mash, or the lauter. Once you have collected the total volume in the kettle your gravity will tell you how efficient your processes are. When brewing a recipe more than once you can track your efficiencies over time, but when you are creating a new recipe there is no prior information to tell you what your Brewhouse Efficiency is. The best way to determine a Brewhouse Efficiency for a new recipe, take a known brewhouse efficiency from a recipe with a similar grain bill.
